Mengqiao

Mengqiao Liu (CN) creates works at the intersection of technology, art, and human experience. Her practice focuses on how people interact with each other through technology, how stories can be told in new ways, and how software and hardware can be woven together to form immersive and interactive environments.

Trained in both engineering and design, she holds a B.Eng. in Information Security and is currently pursuing an MA in New Media at Aalto University, Finland. Alongside her academic practice, she has worked in industry contexts such as BMW and Kuaishou, bridging applied design with technology.

Currently: Creative Technologist Intern @ BMW, Munich.
