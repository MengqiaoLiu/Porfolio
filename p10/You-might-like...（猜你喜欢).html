You might like...(猜你喜欢)

Imagine this scenario: You and your friend were talking about a cute puppy you just saw on the street. Five minutes later, Amazon is recommending dog beds and dog treats to you……

This art installation reveals the hidden mechanisms behind ad recommendations through real-time data monitoring. Visitors can see their data being captured, analyzed, and used to generate personalized ads. The interactive installation highlights issues of data privacy, inviting the audience to reflect on personal information security and commercialization in the digital age.

Category: Interactive Installation

Location: Helsinki, Finland

Year: 2024

Tools: Touch Designer; ChatGPT; ComfyUI
