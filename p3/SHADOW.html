SHADOW

Inspired by social news, SHADOW is committed to calling on society and people to care for and help children who have been sexually abused.

Like the shadows of the devil projected on the wall, the experience of sexual abuse in childhood cannot be erased easily from the victim's mind. SHADOW hopes to show the cruel and terrifying image of the perpetrator and the helplessness of the victim by comparing the participant's own shadow with the shadows of the devil in the projection.

Besides, when more and more people stand together in the face of these shadows, the shadow of the devil will fade and disappear, to express that if more love and help are given to the victims, the shadow in their hearts will dissipate and their hurt will be appeased.

Category: Interactive Installation

Location: Beijing, China

Year: 2020
